Data Science

Experienced Data Scientist and an associate member of the Chartered Institute for IT with strong background in Machine Learning, Big Data Analysis, Process System Engineering, Automation and Advanced Process Control, Fault Tolerant Control Systems, Business Administration and Finance. An experienced University teacher in Chemical Engineering and a Fellow of the Higher Education Academy (FHEA).

I am also a professional member of IEEE with memberships of Control System, Instrumentation and Measurement, Robotics and Automation Societies. My most recent Data Scientist role was as a Research Fellow in Pharmaceutical Process Characterisation and Data Analysis at the School of Chemical and Process Engineering, University of Leeds. The research focus was to resolve issues of sticking in the manufacture of pharmaceutical tablet through Big Data Analysis using several machine learning algorithms. It was a collaboration between the University of Leeds and Pfizer Global Research Sandwich, and part of Advanced Digital Design of Pharmaceutical Therapeutics (ADDoPT) project involving select UK universities, SMEs and the UK pharmaceutical industry.

I am a proactive individual with great team spirit, dynamic teaching and research capabilities and core technical competencies in data-based fault detection and diagnosis using multivariate statistical process monitoring (MSPM) techniques such as dynamic principal component analysis (DPCA) and dynamic partial least squares (DPLS), Process Control, Data Analytics and Fault Tolerant Control Systems.

Some of my skills and competencies are:

  • Excellent application skills in Matlab, Simulink, Python, JavaScript, C++, SQL, Orange and Aspen Hysys.
  • Expertise in multivariate statistical modelling and data analysis using various machine learning algorithms.
  • Proficient in the applications of Spotfire, MS Project, SPSS, MS Words, Excel and Power Point.
  • Great computational, problem-solving, peoples’ management, administrative and time-management skills.
  • Excellent skills in development, calibration and simulation of steady state and dynamic models using both Aspen Hysys and Matlab.
  • Excellent grasp of principles underpinning chemical process dynamics and control, and fault tolerant control.
  • Expertise in advanced process automation and control systems technology.
  • Skilled in the development of PFD, P&ID, material balance and energy balance for chemical processes.
  • Excellent understanding of industrial chemical and manufacturing operations and management.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
